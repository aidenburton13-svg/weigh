Weigh an evidence-weighted priority tool

Origin

Not all evidence is worth the same.

During a product internship at an agtech startup, I ran 79 farmer interviews to find the strongest product idea to pitch. The hard part was never counting how many people mentioned a problem — it was knowing that a farmer's unprompted complaint is worth more than my own guess about what might help them, and that a cheap idea with weak evidence isn't automatically worse than an expensive one with strong evidence. This tool makes that trade-off explicit instead of doing it in my head.

Ranked ideas

Sorted by evidence weight per day of build cost. Highest first.
